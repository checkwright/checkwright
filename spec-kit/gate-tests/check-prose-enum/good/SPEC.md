# Good — complete lists, scattered mentions, and marked subsets

The signals alpha, beta, gamma are the whole set, named in one hand list,
so nothing dropped out.

A lone mention of alpha is not a list; beta turns up in a wholly separate
clause much later in this same paragraph, never adjacent to it, so the two
read as mentions, not an enumeration.

Illustrative subsets mark themselves as partial: signals such as alpha and
beta cover the common path.

A partitive names a selection, not the set: any of alpha, beta may fire first.
